📖 How to Play

The Premise: One player is the Dealer; everyone else is trying to survive. The Dealer sets a trap, and you have time to dodge it.

🃏 Phase 1: The Dealer's Trap

The Dealer chooses a category (the Prompt) and secretly locks in a forbidden answer (the Trap Word).
Example Prompt: "Name a fast food chain" | Trap Word: "McDonald's"
The Dealer also sets a secret Decoy Word to trick the other players during the voting phase.

🏃 Phase 2: The Response

As a Responder, you have two jobs:
1. Survive: Type a Safe Word that fits the Prompt. If you type the Trap Word, you explode and forfeit the round. You must be fast—no two players can lock in the same word!
2. The Bounty (Side-Bet): Once you are safe, try to read the Dealer's mind. Guess their exact Trap Word for a bonus point. (Note: You only get the Bounty point if you survive the whole round.)

⚖️ Phase 3: The Tribunal

All the locked Safe Words are put on the screen anonymously. Players vote to eliminate any words that don't fit the prompt.
⚠️ The Landmine: The Dealer's Decoy Word is hiding in this list! If you vote for the Decoy, you step on a landmine and lose 1 point.

🏆 Scoring Breakdown

  • ✅ Survive: +1 point for dodging the trap and surviving the Tribunal.
  • 🎯 The Bounty: +1 bonus point for guessing the Trap Word.
  • 🍯 The Honeypot: -1 point for voting for the Dealer's Decoy.
  • 💥 Trapped or Vetoed: 0 points for the round.
🧠 Secret Rule (Mind Reader Alliance): If your Safe Word exactly matches the Dealer's Decoy Word, you are granted absolute immunity and survive the round even if the Tribunal votes you out!
