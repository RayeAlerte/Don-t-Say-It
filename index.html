📖 How to Play

The Premise: One player is the Dealer; everyone else is trying to survive. The Dealer sets a trap, and you have time to dodge it.

🃏 Phase 1: The Dealer's Trap

The Dealer chooses a Prompt and secretly locks in a forbidden answer (the Trap Word).
Example: "Name a fast food chain" | Trap Word: "McDonalds"
The Dealer also sets a secret Decoy Word to trick other players during the tribunal.

🏃 Phase 2: The Response

As a Responder, you have two jobs:
Survive: Write Safe Words that fit the Prompt.
If you type the Trap Word, you explode and forfeit the round.
Be quick—no two players can lock in the same word!

Get Them Back: Once you are safe, guess the Dealer's Trap Word for a point bonus.
(Note: You only get the Bounty point if you survive the whole round.)

⚖️ Phase 3: The Tribunal

The Safe Words are shown to the players. Veto any words that don't fit the Prompt.
⚠️ The Dealer's Decoy Word is hiding in this list! If you vote for the Decoy, you lose points.

🏆 Scoring Breakdown

  • ✅ Survive: +1 point for dodging the trap and surviving the Tribunal.
  • 🎯 The Bounty: +1 bonus point for guessing the Trap Word.
  • 🍯 The Honeypot: -1 point for voting for the Dealer's Decoy.
  • 💥 Trapped: 0 points for the round. The Dealer gets 1 point per trap.
  • 💥 Vetoed: 0 points for the round.
🧠 Secret Rule (Mind Reader Alliance): If your Safe Word matches the Dealer's Decoy Word, you are granted absolute immunity and survive the round even if the Tribunal votes you out!
